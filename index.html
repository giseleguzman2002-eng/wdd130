Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires is the capital and largest city of Argentina. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant arts scene, and diverse architecture. The city is famous for its tango music and dance, as well as its delicious cuisine, which includes dishes like empanadas, asado (barbecue), and dulce de leche. Buenos Aires is also home to numerous theaters, museums, and historic landmarks, making it a popular destination for tourists from around the world.
