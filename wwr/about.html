White water rafting has a rich history that dates back to the early 19th century. The sport originated in the United States, where explorers and adventurers would navigate the country's rivers in search of new lands and resources. The first recorded instance of white water rafting took place in 1811, when a group of fur traders used canoes to navigate the rapids of the Missouri River.
